## Store Listing Short Copy

Type a shortcut, get slung to the right place instantly.

Slingshot lets you launch AI prompts and custom search shortcuts from your address bar or search engine page. Search YouTube, Reddit, Wikipedia, developer docs, research sites, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and more with fast DuckDuckGo-style bang commands.

## Store Listing Long Copy

Slingshot is a fast shortcut launcher for search engines and AI tools.

Use simple bang commands like `!yt`, `!r`, `!w`, `!cl`, or `!ai` to jump directly to the site you want. Slingshot works from the browser address bar with the `bg` omnibox keyword and can also intercept supported Google, Bing, and DuckDuckGo searches when your query contains a shortcut.

Good for:

- searching YouTube, Reddit, Wikipedia, GitHub, docs, and research sites faster;
- sending prompts to AI tools without opening each site manually;
- creating custom `%s` search templates for your own workflows;
- opening several AI or search destinations from one query.
