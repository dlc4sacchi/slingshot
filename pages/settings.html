Type a shortcut, get slung to the right place instantly. Here's everything you can do with Slingshot.

04
Shortcuts go anywhere in your query
Put the shortcut at the start, middle, or end. All three work the same way.
start
!cl best pizza in warsaw
middle
best pizza !cl in warsaw
end
best pizza in warsaw !cl
The query sent is always best pizza in warsaw regardless of where the shortcut sits.
